📖 How to Use the Weekly Report

The report has four tabs — click any tab to switch views (your tab choice is saved in the URL so bookmarks and refresh keep your place):

  1. 🔍 Search All — Instant cross-search across every offer (food + non-food). Just type a product name, brand, or store and matching results appear immediately with highlighted matches. Click any column header to sort ascending/descending. Use store chips to filter by store.
  2. 📋 All Offers — Every food offer from all stores this week, grouped by parent & sub-category. Click a category header to collapse / expand. Use the search bar and store chips to filter. Toggle 🥇 Cheapest Only to see just the best currently-available deal per category (future offers are listed but not highlighted). Use 📂 Expand All / 📁 Collapse All to manage sections.
  3. 🏷️ Non-Food — Household, beauty, electronics and other non-food deals. Use the search bar, store chips, and 💰 price range chips (Under €5, €5–€10, €10–€20, €20+) to narrow results.
  4. ⭐ Brand Watchlist — Track your favorite brands and products. Type a name (e.g. "Nutella", "Barilla") to add it to your watchlist. The report fuzzy-matches your watchlist against all current offers each week. Features: summary table with best price/PPU/store at the top, collapse/expand individual items, drag to reorder, import/export as JSON. Click the ⭐ button on any offer row in any tab to add it to your watchlist. Everything is saved on your device and carries over between weekly reports.

🛒 Shopping List:

  • Click + on any offer to add it to your shopping list (click again to remove)
  • Open the 🛒 shopping list drawer (top-right) to see items grouped by store with checkboxes
  • Your list is saved on your device and persists across page refreshes

💡 Tips:

  • The green best-row highlight marks the cheapest currently available offer — future offers are shown but not highlighted
  • The "Valid" column shows when each offer starts and ends (DD-MM → DD-MM format)
  • Use the 🌙/☀️ button (top-right) to toggle dark mode
  • A ↑ scroll-to-top button appears when you scroll down
  • Active filters are shown in a bar below the search — click ✕ to remove individual filters
  • Works great on mobile — tabs collapse to icons, tables scroll sideways
