📖 Quick Guide

The report has four tabs — your tab choice is saved in the URL so bookmarks keep your place:

  1. 🔍 Search All — Search every offer by name, brand, or store. Click column headers to sort. Use store chips to filter.
  2. 📋 All Offers — All food deals grouped by category. Toggle 🥇 Cheapest Only to see the best deal per category.
  3. 🏷️ Non-Food — Household, beauty & electronics deals. Filter by store or price range.
  4. 🏪 Store Optimizer — Select the categories you want to buy and instantly see which single store covers the most items at the best total price. Compares single-store convenience against optimal cherry-picking.
  5. ⭐ Brand Watchlist — Track brands you care about (e.g. "Nutella"). Matching offers appear automatically each week, saved on your device.

💡 Tips:

  • Click + on any offer to add it to your 🛒 shopping list (top-right drawer, grouped by store)
  • Green highlight = cheapest currently-available deal in that category
  • Use 🌙/☀️ (top-right) to toggle dark mode
  • Works great on mobile
