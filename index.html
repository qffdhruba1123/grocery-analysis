📖 How to Use the Weekly Report

The report has four tabs — click any tab to switch views:

  1. 🔍 Search All — Instant cross-search across every offer (food + non-food). Just type a product name, brand, or store and matching results appear immediately. Click any column header to sort the results by that column. Great when you know exactly what you're looking for.
  2. 📋 All Offers — Every food offer from all stores this week, grouped by category (Dairy, Meat, Produce …). Use the search bar to find a specific product, or click a category header to collapse / expand it. Use store chips to filter by store. Toggle "Cheapest Only" to see just the #1 deal per category.
  3. 🏷️ Non-Food — Household, beauty, electronics and other non-food deals, organized into categories. Use the search bar, store chips, and price range filters (Under €5, €5–€10, etc.) to narrow results.
  4. ⭐ Brand Watchlist — Track your favorite brands and products. Type a brand name (e.g. "Nutella", "Barilla", "Coca Cola") to add it to your watchlist. Each week, the report fuzzy-matches your watchlist against all current offers and shows matching results — or tells you there are no offers this week. Your watchlist is saved on your device and persists across reports.

🛒 Shopping List:

  • Click the + button on any offer to add it to your shopping list (click again to remove)
  • Open the shopping list drawer (🛒 icon) to see items grouped by store with checkboxes
  • Your list is saved on your device and persists across page refreshes

💡 Tips:

  • 🟢 Best Deal = near all-time low — stock up!
  • 🟡 Good Price = slightly above average — still worth buying
  • 🔴 Skip = above average — wait for a better week
  • Use the search bar in each tab to find products fast
  • The "Valid" column shows when each offer starts and ends (DD-MM format)
  • Works great on mobile — scroll tables sideways if needed
