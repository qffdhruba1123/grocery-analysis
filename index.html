📖 How to Use the Weekly Report

The report has four tabs — click any tab to switch views:

  1. 🔍 Search All — Instant cross-search across every offer (food + non-food). Just type a product name, brand, or store and matching results appear immediately. Great when you know exactly what you're looking for.
  2. 📋 All Offers — Every food offer from all stores this week, grouped by category (Dairy, Meat, Produce …). Use the search bar to find a specific product, or click a category header to collapse / expand it. Includes price-per-unit so you can compare across pack sizes.
  3. 🏷️ Non-Food — Household, beauty, electronics and other non-food deals, organized into categories. Use the search bar to filter and the expand/collapse buttons to browse by category.
  4. 📊 Weekly Analysis — AI-powered smart shopping list. Products are rated as Best Deal 🟢, Good Price 🟡, or Skip 🔴 based on 5-week price history. Only items that beat or match their historical average are recommended. The Brand Watchlist section tracks specific brands you care about.

💡 Tips:

  • 🟢 Best Deal = near all-time low — stock up!
  • 🟡 Good Price = slightly above average — still worth buying
  • 🔴 Skip = above average — wait for a better week
  • Use the search bar in each tab to find products fast
  • "Valid From" dates tell you when each offer starts
  • Works great on mobile — scroll tables sideways if needed
